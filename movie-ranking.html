The Best Movies According to Michael

My top 3 movies of all time.

The Covenant

This movie was a beautifly told masterpiece of a fictional, but very real period in World history.

John Wick Chapter 4

This movie tied in the previous 3 movies while setting up the John Wick Universe for spinoffs. It captured how hard John Wick is willing to fight for his freedom.

Avatar 2: The way of the water

I liked this movie because it continued from the first one, with stunning visuals and solid action sequences.
